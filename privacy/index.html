WhereTho?

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 3, 2026

WhereTho? ("we", "us", or "our") helps groups decide where to eat. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using the WhereTho? app you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account we collect your email address and a display name. Authentication is handled by Clerk (see Section 4).

Location. When you use the app to find restaurants, we collect your device's GPS coordinates. Location is only sent to our servers when you initiate a restaurant search or join a group session. We do not track your location in the background.

Pick history. We store the restaurants picked for you so you can review past decisions in the app. Each record contains the restaurant name, address, and the date of the pick.

Usage information. Apple may collect crash reports and basic usage analytics through Xcode Organizer and TestFlight. We use this data only to fix bugs and improve stability. We do not use any additional analytics SDKs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To find restaurants near you using the Google Places API
  • To run group sessions where multiple users vote on a restaurant pick
  • To display your pick history within the app
  • To authenticate you securely across sessions
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app performance

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Contract. Processing your email and pick history is necessary to provide the WhereTho? service you signed up for.
  • Consent. We ask for your permission before accessing your device's location. You can withdraw consent at any time in your device's Settings.
  • Legitimate interests. Crash reporting and stability monitoring, balanced against your privacy interests.

4. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third parties only as necessary to operate the service:

  • Clerk — authentication and account management. Clerk receives your email address and manages session tokens. Clerk Privacy Policy
  • Google Places API — restaurant search and details. Your location coordinates are sent server-side to Google to find nearby restaurants. We never send your name, email, or account information to Google. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple — app distribution (App Store / TestFlight) and optional crash reporting via Xcode Organizer. Apple Privacy Policy
  • DigitalOcean — cloud infrastructure. Your data is stored on servers in the United States. DigitalOcean Privacy Policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third party.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Pick history entries are automatically deleted after 365 days. You can also clear your pick history at any time from the Profile screen in the app. If you delete your account, all associated personal data is deleted within 30 days. Crash reports retained by Apple are subject to Apple's own retention schedule.

6. Your Rights

Access Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
Deletion Ask us to delete your account and all associated data.
Correction Ask us to correct inaccurate data we hold about you.
Portability Request your data in a machine-readable format.
Withdrawal of consent Revoke location permission at any time in device Settings.
Objection Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at darodecap@mac.com. We will respond within 30 days. If you are in the EEA and believe we have not addressed your concern, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know. You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete. You may request deletion of personal information we have collected from you.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

We do not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA. To submit a request, contact us at darodecap@mac.com.

8. Children's Privacy

WhereTho? is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We use HTTPS for all data in transit and store data on access-controlled cloud infrastructure. Authentication tokens are managed by Clerk using industry-standard RS256 JWT signing. No security measure is perfect; we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes, we will notify you via the app or email. Continued use of WhereTho? after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
